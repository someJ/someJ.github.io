Information and Performance Services (I+P) is an independent specialist company working within the water sector providing technical and management consultancy, data capture and analysis software and meter commissioning, testing, calibration and verification services.

Consultancy

The team at I+P have extensive knowledge of the management and operation of water and wastewater systems and provide a range of consultancy and training services worldwide in subjects such as operations management, leakage control and flow measurement.

Meter Calibration

Working with some of the world's foremost calibration rigs, I+P can advise, support and manage your calibration needs.

Services

The company has unparalleled knowledge of all major flow metering technologies for pipes and open channel and has specialised in problem solving worldwide.

MeterVue

MeterVue is a web based data analysis tool that collects data from your meters and turns it into management information.

Compatible with a wide range of measuring instruments and utilising established SMS and GPRS networks, MeterVue scales easily from a single meter to a large instrument fleet.
